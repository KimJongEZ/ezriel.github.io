About Me

Ezriel Bornea is a full-time student at San Jose State University. He’s pursuing his bachelor’s degree in business with a concentration in management information systems. He’s a first generation immigrant college student and intends to enter the tech industry after graduation. Ezriel works in collaborative environments to enhance his professional skills. He’s employed in a retail position for a national outdoor and conservation company, he volunteers for his local church organization and is involved with the management information systems association. These experiences have provided him with skills in time management, collaboration and a strong work ethic. Ezriel intends for these skills to help prepare him for his life after college. Outside of work and school, Ezriel’s interests include going to the gym and traveling. He believes that spending time away from home is a good way to open oneself up to new perspectives. He routinely works out and believes that having discipline is a good way to stay focused on his goals.
